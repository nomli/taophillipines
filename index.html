Our Bespoke expeditions are private trips where you can get lost in the islands with your own group of friends or family on a free flowing journey of adventure.

This trip is not for everyone.

It might be bad weather, it might be rough waves. Limited privacy. Barking Dogs, crowing roosters, mosquito bites, jellyfish stings, sunburn. But that is the nature of the Expedition, the unexpected, the unknown, stepping outside your comfort zone. We love it, but we understand that this is not everyone's idea of a holiday.
